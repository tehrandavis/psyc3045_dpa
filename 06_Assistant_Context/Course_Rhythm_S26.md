## The Core Cadence: "Primer-Reveal"
The course runs on a 2-part weekly cycle designed to leverage the small seminar format (N=4). We move from **experience** (Thursday) to **theory** (Tuesday).

---

## üìÖ The Standard Week

### **MONDAY (Prep & Reading)**
*   **Student:**
    *   Finishes the weekend reading (Blau & Wagman + Articles).
    *   Takes the **Reading Quiz** on Canvas (Opens Fri 5PM, Due Tue 9AM).
*   **Instructor:**
    *   Reviews Reading Quiz results (Monday night or Tuesday morning) to identify "Muddiest Points."
    *   Finalizes Tuesday Seminar slides based on Week 1 Lab feedback.

### **TUESDAY (The Reveal: Seminar)**
*   **09:00 AM:** Reading Quiz Due.
*   **Class (75 min):**
    *   **0:00 - 0:10:** Quiz Debrief (Address misconceptions).
    *   **0:10 - 0:40:** **The Reveal (Lecture):** Connects last Thursday's mystery to this week's theory. "Here is the math/concept that explains what you felt."
    *   **0:40 - 0:75:** Seminar Discussion (Driven by student "Real World Challenges" or Reading prompts).
    *   **Minute Paper:** "What is still muddy?" (Exit Ticket).

### **WEDNESDAY (The Gap)**
*   **Student:** Light review or work on long-term projects (Thought Papers/Curation).
*   **Instructor:**
    *   Reviews Minute Papers.
    *   Preps materials for Thursday Lab (e.g., gathers props, prints worksheets).

### **THURSDAY (The Primer: Data & Experience)**
*   **Class (75 min):**
    *   **0:00 - 0:15:** **Field Notes:** Round-robin sharing of real-world perception examples.
    *   **0:15 - 0:45:** **The Activity (The Mystery):** Hands-on demo (e.g., Wielding, Stair Lab). Students collect data/observations. **No theory yet.**
    *   **0:45 - 0:65:** **The Empirical Snapshot:** Review key figures/results from a classic paper (e.g., Warren, 1984). Compare student data to the "Official Science."
    *   **0:65 - 0:75:** **Lab Worksheet:** Students synthesize observations and file data into their Lab Dossier.
*   **Assignment:**
    *   Instructor assigns the **Next Reading** (for next Tuesday).
    *   Instructor assigns the **Real World Challenge** (observation task for next Tuesday).

### **FRIDAY (Deliverables)**
*   **Student:**
    *   Major Due Date (When applicable): Thought Papers, Curation Check-ins.
    *   Starts Reading for next week.
*   **Instructor:**
    *   Grades Lab Dossier items (Worksheets/Notes) from Thursday (Low stakes, check for completion).
