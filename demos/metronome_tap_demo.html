What to Explore
  • Preferred Rate: Most people's natural tapping tempo falls between 80–160 BPM. What's yours?
  • Variability: The standard deviation tells you how consistent your tapping is. Lower = more stable.
  • Connection to HKB: Your preferred rate maps to the reference frequency (ω₀) in the HKB model. In the bimanual tapping demo, you can experience the phase transition firsthand by speeding up past ~1.3× your preferred rate.
