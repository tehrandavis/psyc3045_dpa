What to Notice
  • The b/a Ratio governs the strength of the coordination attractors. At 1.0, Anti-phase is strongly stable.
  • Speeding up past 1.3× preferred frequency forces b/a below 0.25 (the bifurcation point).
  • Critical Fluctuations: Right before the transition, you will see the relative phase wobble violently due to the noise term overcoming the weakening attractor basin.
  • Phase Transition: The system snaps from Anti-phase to In-phase. It cannot transition back even if you slow down (hysteresis).
