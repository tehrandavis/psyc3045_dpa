What to Explore
  • Anti-phase tapping: Alternate F-J-F-J at a comfortable speed. φ should hover around 180°.
  • In-phase tapping: Press F and J at the same time. φ should be near 0° (or 360°).
  • Speed up: Gradually increase the metronome BPM while tapping anti-phase. At some critical speed, you may involuntarily switch to in-phase — a phase transition.
  • Variability: Watch the SD of φ. It should increase right before a transition — these are critical fluctuations, a hallmark of the HKB model.
  • Hysteresis: After switching to in-phase, try slowing back down. Can you return to anti-phase, or does in-phase persist?
