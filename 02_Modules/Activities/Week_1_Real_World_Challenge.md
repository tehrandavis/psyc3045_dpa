### 3. The Stress Test
*Why does this model fail for this specific task? Pick **ONE** trap and apply it.*

**[ ] Trap A: The Speed Limit (Processing Time)**
*   How fast does the event happen? ______________
*   If perception takes ~200ms, where would you be by the time you finished calculating? (e.g., "I would have already crashed," "The ball would have hit my face").
    <br>

**[ ] Trap B: The Ambiguity (Poverty of Stimulus)**
*   Is the static input ambiguous? (e.g., "A small car close up looks the same as a big car far away").
*   Does the robot need to "guess" or "infer" what is real? How could it guess wrong?
    <br>

**[ ] Trap C: The Homunculus (Infinite Regress)**
*   Does the robot need a "rule" to interpret the input? (e.g., "If dark patch, then hole").
*   How does it know *that* rule applies here? (e.g., "What if it's just a shadow?").
