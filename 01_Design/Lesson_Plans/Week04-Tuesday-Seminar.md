## ðŸ•’ Activity 3: Core Activity - "Mapping the Array" (00:35 - 01:00)

**Setup:** Students stand in different parts of the room.

**Step 1: The Unique Point of Observation**
> "Everyone freeze. Look at your view.
> Is anyone else seeing *exactly* what you are seeing?
> No. Your view is unique to your point in space-time."

**Step 2: The Flow Field**
> "Now walk forward. What happens to the texture on the floor?"
> *(It flows out).*
> "What happens to the texture at the horizon?"
> *(It stays still).*
>
> "This is **Global Optical Flow**. It specifies YOUR movement, not the world's movement."

**Step 3: The Array vs. The Image**
> "Why does Gibson hate the word 'Image'?
> Because an image is static. The Array is dynamic. It exists even if you aren't looking at it."
