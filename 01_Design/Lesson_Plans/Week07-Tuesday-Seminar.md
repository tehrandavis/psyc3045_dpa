## ðŸ•’ Activity 3: Core Activity - "The Stability Landscape" (00:35 - 01:00)

**Setup:** Visualize the "Ball in the Valley" metaphor.

**Step 1: Point Attractor**
> "A marble in a bowl. It always settles at the bottom.
> Example: Reaching for a coffee cup. Your hand always ends up at the cup."

**Step 2: Limit Cycle Attractor**
> "A planet orbiting a star. It never stops, but it never leaves the path.
> Example: Walking. Breathing."

**Step 3: Repeller**
> "The top of a hill. You can balance a ball there, but if you breathe on it, it runs away.
> Example: Trying to walk *exactly* at the transition speed between walk/run."
