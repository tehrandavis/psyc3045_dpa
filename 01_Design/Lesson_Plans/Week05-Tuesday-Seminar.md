**Setup:** We need to break the Subjective/Objective divide.

**Step 1: Objective vs. Subjective**
> "Physics is Objective (Mass, Length).
> Psychology is Subjective (Value, Meaning).
> Where do Affordances fit?"

**Step 2: The Ecological Patch**
> "Gibson says Affordances are **Both**. Or **Neither**.

> *Example:* 'Edibility' is a fact of biology (Objective), but it describes value to me (Subjective).
>
> **Discussion:** Can an affordance exist if no one is there to perceive it?
